Our core Features

Products and listings Users & Profiles Payment Getaways Orders management Search and Filters Email Notifications Mobile Friendly

Products & Listings

Add and manage all your products and listings from one admin panel.

Include multiple images and detaled descriptions of your products like available colors, material, size weight and many more. Users can see all the information about your product, add it to their cart and checkout saving you the hassle of always answering the same questions to customers who end up not buying the product!

See Demo

Users & Profiles

Have your users/buyers create and manage their own profiles to get a more engaging experience.

Users can create profiles to save things like their addresses and details to avoid them repeating all the time when they come back to buy more products. They also have a wishlist where they can save products they plan on buying later! User profiles are associated with their current carts meaning they can hop from a laptop to a mobile phone and not lose their items added to cart

See Demo

Payment Getaways

Setup payment getaways like Mpesa or Paypal to ensure smooth secure and trustworthy transactions

You can link your shop to your Mpesa paybill number for easier tracking of funds. Have your buyers check out with Mpesa to complete an order to avoid you making a delivery only for the buyer to decline buying the product. This also eliminates the suspision of your shop being a con as the customers will be able to see your registred business name matches your shop/business name. More payment getaways can be arranged on request

See Demo

Orders Management

Manage all your orders and payments from one admin panel

You can manage all your orders and payments from your admin panel. You will receive notifications once an order has been sucessfully made and confirmed. You can list all your orders and payments down, view details of the order and make the necessary arrangements for your products to reach the buyer. You can also filter through orders and payments by status, amount or date created

See Demo

Search and filters

Users can filter through your products by price & category and search for specific ones by keywords

Users can find your products easily by searching for them by keywords in their name, description or category. They can also apply filters like price, newness, category or promotions. This search and filters functionality also applies in the admin panel making it easier for you(Admin) to find orders, payments, deliveries or even users.

See Demo

Email notifications

Both you(admin) and Users get email notifications for the events that occur on the site

Once an order has been succefully placed, both the admin and the user recieves an email notification on the order. When the order status changes(Items shipped/ready for pick up, order successfully completed) The user also recieves notification on the same. The user also recives an invoice/receipt one a transaction was successful and order was succesfully completed.

SMS notifications can also be arranged upon request

See Demo

Mobile Friendly

The website is fully mobile responsive

Your online shop will be fully responsive and users can enjoy its awesome design and functionality from any device without having to struggle to find things. The online shop is compatible with mobile phones of any size, tablets, laptops and even desktops.
